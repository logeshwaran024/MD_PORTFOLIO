[ PROFESSIONAL OVERVIEW ]

My professional journey began in the engineering industry, where I gained valuable experience in manufacturing, technical operations, sales, and business development. These early roles helped me develop strong problem-solving abilities, leadership skills, and a deep understanding of business systems.

Driven by an entrepreneurial mindset, I explored multiple ventures before founding ECOG Products in 2020. Over the years, I expanded into technology and organizational development, building businesses focused on sustainability, innovation, people development, and long-term value creation.

Driven by an entrepreneurial mindset, I explored multiple ventures before founding ECOG Products in 2020. Over the years, I expanded into technology and organizational development, building businesses focused on sustainability, innovation, people development, and long-term value creation.

These experiences shaped my entrepreneurial mindset and inspired me to create meaningful impact through business. From engineering and business development to founding multiple ventures, every step has strengthened my commitment to sustainability, innovation, leadership, and empowering people to reach their full potential.

My business journey was built on a foundation of discipline, continuous learning, and practical experience. Working across engineering, manufacturing, sales, and business development provided valuable insights into operations, customer needs, and sustainable growth strategies.

These experiences laid the groundwork for entrepreneurship and helped shape a system-driven approach to leadership. By combining innovation, strong business processes, and people development, I have focused on building organizations that create long-term value, drive positive impact, and support sustainable growth.

My vision extends beyond building successful businesses. It is about creating sustainable solutions, empowering people, and developing organizations that generate lasting value for society. Every initiative is guided by a commitment to innovation, responsibility, and continuous improvement.

Through purpose-driven leadership and strong systems, I aim to build businesses that create opportunities, support communities, and inspire future generations. The focus remains on achieving meaningful growth while making a positive environmental and social impact.
